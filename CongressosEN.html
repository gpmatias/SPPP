Society for Affective Science Inaugural Conference

The Inaugural Conference will be held between April 24th and 26th, 2014 in Washington, D.C., USA. This conference will promote basic and applied research in the variety of fields - from psychology, to medicine and computer science - that study affect. Furthermore, it is an opportunity for researchers and other professionals to share knowledge. For further information, please refer to the Society for Affective Science website.
