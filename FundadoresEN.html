Assistant Professor (tenured) at the Psychology School, University of Minho, Portugal. She received her Doctorate in Psychology (Social Psychology) from the University of Minho. She currently teaches undergraduate and graduate students, mentoring Master's and Doctoral students. Her research focuses in the psico-socio-physiological processes associated with subjective experience and optimal functioning. She is part of national and international groups and has published in scientific journals and books.

In her practice, she develops individual and group interventions with children and adolescents. She implements intervention programs that aim to promote positive development and well-being. Presently, she is the Director of the Psychology Service (ServPsi) at the School of Psychology, University of Minho, Portugal. She is a member of the Executive Comitee and Portuguese representative of the European Network for Positive Psychology – ENPP.
