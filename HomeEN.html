The aim of the Portuguese Positive Psychology (SPPP) is to research and promote scientifical work in the Positive Psychology domain. The SPPP aims to support the production of knowledge in this domain, thus promoting scientific investigation in subjects within this field. In this website, you will find information regarding Positive Psychology, such as: national and international congresses, scientific articles, courses, and others.


Positive Psychology: a brief introduction

The Positive Psychology "movement" started when Martin Seligman became president of the American Psychological Association (APA). In the same year, Martin Seligman and Mihalyi Csikszentmihalyi published a special edition of the American Psychologist dedicated to Positive Psychology. In the first paper, Seligman and Csikszentmihalyi highlighted the importance of a shift in paradigm in psychology: from an remedial approach, to an approach centered in the strengths and qualities of individuals and societies (Seligman & Csikszentmihalyi, 2000).

Presently, research and intervention in Positive Psychology have developed, crossing several other psychology domains, and expanding to countries all around the world.
