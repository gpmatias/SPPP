Books

The best within us: Positive Psychology perspectives on eudaimonia

Alan S. Waterman | 2013

Several authors and researchers whose work has been central to understanding the positive aspects of psychological function contributed to this book.This book is for social and personality psychologists with interest in teaching and research in positive psychology. This book is of interest to psychologists, philosophers, sociologists, political scientists, and graduate students who are interested in research in this field.

Teresa Freire, researcher and assistant professor at the Psychology School, University of Minho, and international colleagues, published the chapter "Cross-Cultural Perceptions of Meaning and Goals in Adulthood: Their Roots and Relations with Happiness".

Positive Leisure Science. From subjective experience to social contexts

Teresa Freire | 2012

Several distinguished authors in the leisure domain contributed to this book. This book's topics include cognitive and behavioral optimization processes through leisure, locations and contexts of leisure, and social and cultural role of leisure. The aim of this book is to extend the Positive Psychology constructs to different scientific domains, highlighting the crucial role of leisure in individual and social processes that underlie optimal human functioning.

The Wiley-Blackwell Handbook of Psychology of Coaching and Mentoring

Jonathan Passmore, David Peterson, e Teresa Freire | 2012

Several distinguished authors present, in 26 chapters, theoretical models and research findings about coaching and mentoring through a detailed review of literature. This book is a reference for Coaching and Mentoring Psychology and it it a manual for researchers, professores, scientists, and students in the field.

Adaptação e Bem Estar nas Escolas Portuguesas

Luis Picado e Alexandra Marques Pinto | 2012

This books approaches the themes of school adaptation and well-being by presenting a set of studies performed among portuguese students - pre-school, first to third graders, highschoolers, undergraduate students and graduate and military students. This books contributes to the understanding of the adaption processes in portuguese schools, through the lenses of its main characters: the students and teachers.

Joana S. Carvalho e Alexandra Marques Pinto publish the chapter "Savoring: Uma Forma de Promover o Bem-Estar Pessoal em Adolescentes, Alunos do Ensino Secundário."

C. Figueira, Alexandra Marques Pinto, e M. L. Lima publish the chapter "Adaptação e Bem-estar no Ensino"

A. Gil e Alexandra Marques Pinto publish the chapter "Criatividade e Coping na Explicação do Bem-Estar Subjectivo"

Novos desafios para a Gestão de Recursos Humanos: O caso dos trabalhadores temporários

Maria José Chambel | 2011

The main aim of this book is to report the findings of the project «Relação de emprego nos trabalhadores temporários: As implicações da gestão de recursos humanos», which was developed by the authors between 2010 and 2011 with funding from the Fundação para a Ciência e Tecnologia. The book presents an analysis and reflection about the professional life of temporary workers.

Alexandra Marques Pinto e F. Castanheira publish the chapter "Experiências de trabalho positivas e bem-estar".

Understanding Positive Life. Research and Practice on Positive Psychology

Teresa Freire | 2008

This book presents insightful knowledge about the practive, theory, and research in the Positive Psychology domain. The book is divided in three parts: (1) Happiness, well-being and life satisfaction; (2) Performance, coping strategies and quality of life; and (3) Assessment, interventions and practice for a good life. Each part includes eight chapters published by distinguished authors.
