Members

New members are admitted by proposal by an existing members or self proposal. All applications are evaluated by the Admission Comittee.

SPPP membership:


Member

Full membership is given to those who adhere to all statutes and rules of the Portuguese Positive Psychology Society.

Associate

This option is for those who are not accepted as full members, but who wish to participate in certain activities.

Students

All university students interested in Positive Psychology might be part of the SPPP, according to its statutes and regulations.

Honorary Member

This membership is given by the General Assembly to individuals with distinguished scientific and social contributions.


Applying to become a member

To become a SPPP member, please fill out the Application Form and send it by email or mail.

After receiving the confirmation of acceptance as a member, please make the payment relative to the dues:

  • Full members/Associate members - 24€/year
  • Students (Bachelor's/M.A.) - 12€/year
