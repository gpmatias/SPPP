Mission

The Portuguese Positive Psychology Society (SPPP) is a scientific society founded and certified by law on January, 2010. The aim of the SPPP is to promote scientific work in the Positive Psychology domain through the organization of courses, conferences, and congresses. Furthermore, the SPPP aims to support scientifical publishing in the national and international contexts. Our mission is to support and advance the production of knowledge in this domain.
