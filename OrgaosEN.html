Board and Elections

The Portuguese Positive Psychology Society (SPPP) is a scientific society founded and certified by law on January 2010. We have established the protocolsregulations that regulate it in the SPPP protocol. In the initial phase, the society is governed by the interim board. The first elections for the board were on February 13th, 2012.
